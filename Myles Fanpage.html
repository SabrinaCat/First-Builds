Myles Miller, The Artist To Watch in 2020


Bio

Myles Miller was born in El Paso, Texas. His introduction into music started at the age of 4, when he began to learn piano. Since then, he has focused mainly on string instruments such as the guitar and viola. However he has never strayed from an appreciation of all instruments. He currently records in his private Austin, Texas studio where he is making progress on his new album, Wanderlust.

Proficiency in the following:

  • Piano
  • Guitar
  • Cello
  • Viola

Miller in his Austin, Texas studio


Blended Sounds

Myles Miller's music takes influence from mid 70s icons such as Jimmy Hendrix. The inspiration of whom can be heard in many of his songs. However several tracks feature the folk indie sound that come with the territory of living in Austin, Texas. Its the energy of 70's rock with the tenderness of folk indie all wrapped into one.
